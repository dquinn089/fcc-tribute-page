Spike Spiegel

The iconic bounty hunter from Cowboy Bebop

Here's a timeline of Spike Spiegel's life:

  • 2044 - Born on Mars.
  • 2064 - Joins the Red Dragon Crime Syndicate, showcasing his exceptional combat skills.
  • 2067 - Fakes his death and leaves the Syndicate after falling in love with Julia.
  • 2068 - Partners with Jet Black and begins working as a bounty hunter aboard the Bebop.
  • 2071 - Encounters Faye Valentine, Edward, and Ein, forming an unconventional but effective team.
  • 2071 - Faces his past when Vicious, his former Syndicate partner, rises to power.
  • 2071 - Confronts Vicious in a final battle to avenge Julia and bring closure to his tumultuous past.

"I'm not going there to die. I'm going to find out if I'm really alive."

-- Spike Spiegel
